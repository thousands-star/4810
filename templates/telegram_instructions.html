Telegram Authentication Instructions

To authenticate via Telegram, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Telegram app on your phone or computer.
  2. Search for our bot by typing "@YourBotName" in the search bar.
  3. Send the following message to the bot: /start.
  4. Follow the instructions provided by the bot to complete your authentication.

If you have any issues, please contact our support team.
